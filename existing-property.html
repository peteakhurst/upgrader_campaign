Can we keep our property
as an investment?

Unlike a home, buying the right investment property is purely a financial decision, if you’re emotionally attached to your property you need to re-set. It’s important now to think like an investor and understand the goals and strategies behind a successful property investment.

First, work out exactly what you want to get out of your newly appointed investment property and than assess if it’s a winner.

Secondly consider your position if you didn’t keep the property and had the funds in the bank…would you:


  • a) By a different new type of new home (more bedrooms, better location)
  • b) Buy a different investment property and a new home

We can work alongside you and coach you through the steps to understand if your existing property is a keeper!
