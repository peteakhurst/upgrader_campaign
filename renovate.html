Buy or renovate?

If you’re at the point where your home no longer meets your needs, you face one of life’s big decisions: renovate or relocate.

Whether you’re better off moving house or renovating is completely down to your individual values, needs and finances and of course how much mess you can handle!

Both options present their challenges and headaches, dealing with tradesmen in your home, packing up your lifework of belongings and the fear of the unknown. There are many catalysts for stress around such a decision.

We are here to help guide you and assist you in feeling confident in your decision, whatever it may be.
