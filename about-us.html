About Us


Professional Partners are a boutique mortgage brokerage firm situated in the heart of the Melbourne CBD, specialising in servicing the professional market.

Founding directors, Chartered Accountant Sean Herman and Actuary Craig Schafner bring an extraordinary level of financial expertise to their firm. Since doors opened in 2004 Sean and Craig have established a notable market share in their professional niche. Their first hand understanding of the drivers, motivations and lifestyles of the market has propelled business throughout the last 12 years.

Professional Partners are unswervingly committed to each client and are passionate about building long term relationships. They believe in acting with good will and integrity at every turn.

We are passionate about helping our clients and our track record shows it!
